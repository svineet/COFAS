Initiatives

Here is a list of initiatives from all arround the world in the field of "Peace Through Technology"

  1. BuildPeace 2015

    Build Peace explores how information and communications technologies, games, networking platforms and other technology tools can enhance the impact of a broad range of peacebuilding, social cohesion and peace advocacy initiatives.The conference and community bring together local peacebuilders and technologists to re-think approaches to early warning and crisis response, attitude and behavior change, collaboration, dialogue, and policy advocacy.


  2. World Peace Through Technology (WPTTO)

    The World Peace Through Technology Organization is an educational non-profit dedicated to inspiring and educating people about peace. By raising awareness and knowledge of each other we are promoting global harmony and peace.


  3. Technology For Peace (Cyprus)

    Technology for Peace is a Cyprus-based peoples' initiative launched in 1995 to serve the needs of peace pioneers on the divided island.
